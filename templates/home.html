Recent news about Sport Technology

We watched the latest innovations about Sport from september 2016 to march 2017, with the objective of sharing what's going on in this domain, what will it become in the future and what people think about it.
We divided the news in 5 categories :

  • Connected Sport Devices
  • Mobile apps
  • E-sport
  • Virtual Reality

Timeline

September

Fitbit launches Flex 2 and Charge 2

01/09/2016

Well known connected gear brand Fitbit launches two new products : Flex 2 and Charge 2. They both track your daily life data (sleeping, walking, general activity...) and each one has a specificity : Charge 2 is focused on improving your running skills, whereas Flex 2 is made for tracking yout swimming sessions.

Coros, the smart helmet

06/09/2016

Coros is a smart cycling helmet that allows bikers to ride more safely and more easily. Many features are included in this helmet (headphones, microphone, voice GPS, data tracking, emergency systems), they are accessible throught a remote on the handlebar and improve your biking activities.

Tomtom new tracker reads your body

07/09/2016

Tomtom enters in the connected device market by releasing a new fitness tracker that features body composition reading : the only that guesses the amount of muscles and fat in your body in real-time.

October

Zepp Soccer, track your stats like your FIFA players

17/10/2016

The connected gear brand Zepp has launched a new product for footballers : Zepp Soccer. It's a soccer socket that tracks your data during the play : kicks, sprints and distance are recorded and visualised to help improve your performances.

November

Mio Global, smarter than smart devices

04/11/2016

"It's not the number of steps that counts, it's how much heart you put into it". Mio Global is a connected wristband that gives a score to your daily activity, based on your heart rate only. It claims to be smarter than the other connected devices because the activity tracker is "more intelligent and makes sense of heart rate data".
